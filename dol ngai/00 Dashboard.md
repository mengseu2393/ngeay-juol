# 🏠 RentWise Dashboard

Welcome to the **RentWise** Knowledge Base. This dashboard provides a high-level view of the application features, modules, and current development status.

## 🗺️ Feature Map

Below are the core modules that power the application. Each link leads to its specific feature document describing the rules, flows, and relevant database tables.

- 🔑 **[[Features/Authentication|Authentication & Role Access]]**: Access control for Platform Staff, Landlords, Managers, and Tenants.
- 🏢 **[[Features/Property|Property Management]]**: Creating properties, setting utility rates, and tracking metadata.
- 🛏️ **[[Features/Rental|Tenancy & Leases]]**: Activating/ending leases, tracking room status, and validating dates.
- 👤 **[[Features/Tenant|Tenant Portal]]**: Tenant invoice view, receipts, and username-based logins.
- 💵 **[[Features/Payment|Invoicing & Payments]]**: Generating monthly invoices, tracking payments, and processing PDF/Excel exports.
- 🔧 **[[Features/Maintenance|Maintenance Requests]]**: Submitting and tracking maintenance issues with message logs.
- 🔔 **[[Features/Notification|Notifications]]**: Internal messages, logs, and system events.
