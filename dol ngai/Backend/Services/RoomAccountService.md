# ⚙️ RoomAccountService

- **File Path**: [RoomAccountService.php](file:///home/tms/Desktop/dol-ngai/rentwise/app/Services/RoomAccountService.php)
- **Namespace**: `App\Services`

---

## 🎯 Purpose
Manages username-based accounts and login credentials. It supports two modes of tenant access: permanent room-specific accounts or distinct per-occupant logins.

---

## ⚙️ Responsibilities & Behaviors
- **Login Generation**: Mints unique usernames for room accounts (e.g. `riverside-101`) or individual tenancies (e.g. `101-john-doe`).
- **Idempotent Setup**: Generates room-specific accounts only if they don't already exist.
- **Credential Rotation**: Generates easy-to-read random passwords (alphanumeric, no confusing symbols) to deliver to moving occupants.

---

## 🛠️ Public Methods & Algorithms

### `createForUnit(Unit $unit, ?string $password = null): array`
- **Logic**: Creates a permanent user with role `tenant` mapped to the [[Backend/Models/Unit|Unit]] identifier.

### `createForRental(Rental $rental, ?string $password = null): array`
- **Logic**: Creates a distinct user with role `tenant` mapped directly to the [[Backend/Models/Rental|Rental]] lease. If the rental already has a dedicated account, resets the password.

### `resetPassword(Unit $unit, ?string $password = null): ?array`
- **Logic**: Changes the password on the permanent room account.

---

## 🛡️ Business Rules
1. **Tenant Portability**: Distinct rental accounts preserve occupant history and ensure that a tenant who moves to a new room gets new credentials while their historical bills remain linked to their user account.
2. **Password Casting**: Plain text passwords generated by the service are hashed at database saving using Laravel's native casts.
