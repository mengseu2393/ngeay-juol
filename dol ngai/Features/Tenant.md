# 👤 Feature Spec: Tenant Portal

- **Context Link**: [[Features/Tenant]]
- **Associated Models**: [[Backend/Models/User]], [[Backend/Models/TenantProfile]], [[Backend/Models/Invoice]]

---

## 🎯 Feature Purpose
Enables tenants to log into a read-only portal to view unpaid invoices, read PDF statements, print receipts, and upload payment proof.

---

## ⚙️ Key Operations & Flows
- **Credential Handshake**: Tenants log in using the username/password generated by the landlord.
- **Invoice Overview**: Displays outstanding and historical balances.
- **Payment Log Upload**: Allows uploading bank/mobile transaction transfer receipts.

---

## 🛡️ Business Rules
- **No Back-office Access**: Tenants are strictly prohibited from entering `/admin` or `/landlord` panels.
- **Tenant Isolation Bounds**: Tenants are restricted to invoices where `tenant_id` matches their authenticated ID.
- **Subscription Expiry Tolerance**: Tenants can access their portal even if their landlord's SaaS subscription has expired (tenant data access is protected).
